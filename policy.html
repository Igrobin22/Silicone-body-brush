Store Policies

Clear terms for buying PureForm.

This page gathers the key delivery, returns, refund, privacy, and website terms in one place so customers can understand the purchase before ordering.

Last Updated

Delivery

Shipping & cash on delivery

Orders are prepared after customer confirmation. Delivery times can vary by city, courier availability, and stock status. If cash on delivery is offered, the customer should provide accurate contact and address details so the courier can complete the delivery.

Returns

15-day return window

Unused products may be returned within 15 days of delivery if they are in original condition and packaging. Because this is a personal care product, opened or used items may not be returnable unless they arrive damaged or incorrect.

Refunds

Inspection before refund

Returned items are checked before a refund or replacement is approved. Approved refunds are processed back through the original payment method when possible, or by another agreed method for cash on delivery orders.

Privacy

Customer data

PureForm collects only the details needed to process orders, respond to customer messages, and improve the shopping experience. Customer information should not be sold to unrelated third parties.

Terms

Website use

Product details, pricing, offers, and availability may change. Customers should review the final order information before confirming a purchase. Website content may not be copied or reused without permission.

Support

Questions before ordering

For questions about product use, delivery, returns, or order status, use the contact page and include the order name, phone number, and delivery city where relevant.

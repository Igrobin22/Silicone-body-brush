FAQ

Care, usage, and orders.

  • What comes in the set?The set includes a long handle back scrubber, bath body brush, scalp massager, and hexagonal face brush.
  • Can I use the brushes every day?Yes, the silicone bristles are designed for regular use. Use light pressure, especially on the face and scalp.
  • How do I clean the brushes?Rinse thoroughly with warm water after each use and leave them in an open-air place to dry.
  • Is the face brush separate from the body brush?Yes. The face brush is smaller and softer for delicate facial areas.
  • Do you offer cash on delivery?The site is set up for cash on delivery messaging. Confirm availability for your city before finalizing the order.
  • Can I return the set?Unused sets may be returned within 15 days. Opened personal care products may only be eligible if damaged or incorrect.
